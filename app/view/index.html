WHAT SNEAKER IS THAT?

Upload a photo of a Jordan by clicking below, and we'll tell you what model it is! It currently classifies 13 Air Jordan models (1-13).

The model is trained on ~2k total images and achieves 93% accuracy on the validation set, though the dataset has some duplicates from the image collection process, so this percentage is on the optimistic side.
