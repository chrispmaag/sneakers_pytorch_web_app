WHAT SNEAKER IS THAT?

Upload a photo of a Nike sneaker by clicking below, and we'll tell you what it is!

It currently classifies 24 different Nike sneakers (Air Jordan 1-15, Air Max 1, Air Max 90, Air Max 95, Air Max 97, Air Max 270, Epic React, Vapormax, Pegasus 35, and Foamposite).

The model returns the top three predicted sneakers along with their probabilities.

The model is trained on ~4k total images and achieves 91% accuracy on the validation set, though the dataset has some duplicates from the image collection process, so this percentage is on the optimistic side.
